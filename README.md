NetLog 僅收集和處理以下資料：

# NetLog Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

The NetLog application ("Application") is developed by Fractal Studio.

This privacy policy describes how our application processes your personal data.

NetLog only collects and processes the following data:
- Internet speed measurement results (download, upload, ping)
